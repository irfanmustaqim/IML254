Archery

Introduction to my hobby

  • Peace be upon you,

    • My name is Irfan and my hobby is archery. Here I will tell you how I got involved in the sport of traditional archery and my excitement for this sport so much that I made it my daily hobby during my spare time.

    I became interested in traditional archery as early as the age of 14 in my place called Kg Tempoyang, Kuala Lipis, Pahang. Currently, the mosque in the area where I sit has opened an archery club called Kelab Memanah Masjid Saiyidina Ali Kg Tempoyang. I started joining and registered as a member of the club at the same age. I joined at the invitation of my friend who told me to join him in the archery club. Currently I am a bit clumsy to archery using the bow and arrows provided by the mosque but after a few months of practicing with a skilled teacher, I started to get used to it until I was able to participate in the archery competition organized by the archery club in another village mosque.

    I started participating in my first competition at the age of 15 during the year 2019 at Kg Relong Mosque. At that time I played with only 6 arrows. I play with a mosque bow which is a bit clumsy to use for beginners but I'm used to it. The distance I played in this competition was 18 meters. The result was that I got the fifth place out of 20 participants and got a bronze medal in this competition.

    My second competition was for the same age which is 15 years old in August 2019. This time the competition was organized by my own village mosque and I had registered in the under 15 category. Fortunately in this competition the bow and arrow used the property of my village mosque. I performed well in the competition with 6 arrows. The final result is that I have won first place among 15 contestants. From this victory my grandfather gifted me with a new bow and arrow for myself.

    In conclusion, there are many more archery competitions that I have participated in, but I have forgotten all of them and lack photos when I participated to be used as proof. I am very happy to be able to participate in the competition that I participated in because I can get some knowledge from skilled archers and it can improve my skills in archery. I also really love this sport of archery because it is the sunnah of the Prophet Muhammad SAW. I hope to maintain my momentum in playing this sport.
